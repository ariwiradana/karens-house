About Karéns House

Located 5 km from Neka Art Museum, Karen House Ubud provides accommodation with a garden, a terrace and a 24-hour front desk for your convenience. All units feature air conditioning and a flat-screen TV. A continental breakfast is available daily at the bed and breakfast. If you would like to discover the area, cycling is possible in the surroundings.

Blanco Museum is 6 km from Karen House Ubud, while Saraswati Temple is 6 km away. The nearest airport is Ngurah Rai International, 34 km from the accommodation, and the property offers a paid airport shuttle service.
